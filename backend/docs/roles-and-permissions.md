Smart OPEX defines only two user roles:

## 1. PIC
PIC has full access to all operational features in the system, including:
- Creating and managing activities
- Inputting transaction data
- Uploading all required documents
- Reviewing OCR results
- Viewing dashboards and status summaries
- Exporting final data to Excel (XLSX)

## 2. Verifikator
Verifikator has all permissions granted to PIC, plus additional administrative capabilities:
- Create PIC accounts
- Edit PIC accounts
- Delete PIC accounts

## Important Notes
- There is no operational difference between PIC and Verifikator roles.
- The system does not perform final approval or rejection of transactions.
- Verifikator privileges are limited to user (PIC) management only.
